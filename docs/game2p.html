Zombie Escape

Back

This game is a local 2 player game where both players must work together to escape an endless hoard of zombies. Both players have access to a double jump and wall jump. Its up to both players to avoid the zombies and reach the end of the level. However, there are many obstacles in the way. These can consist of simple jumping puzzles to complex button orders. During all this, there are hordes of zombies and even special zombies that have abilities such as, flight or the ability to move at unbelievable speeds.
